What is programming/coding

A program is a set of instructions given to a machine/computer. (Computer) Programming is the writing of instructions for computers.

You can communicate such instructions in a variety of ways:

  • by souldering wires and transisters in a particular way,
  • or by typing some 1's and 0's and let a machine intepret that,
  • or by writing higher level programming languages like the ones you hear about: C, Java, Python, etc, which somehow get converted into 1's and 0's and then are intepretted by the machine.
